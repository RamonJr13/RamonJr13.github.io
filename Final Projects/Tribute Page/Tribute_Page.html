Jayford Galgalo

The Joker

Here's the time line of Jayford 'Talog' Galgalo:

  • 2001 - Born in Guinzadan Norte, Bauko, Mountain Province.
  • 2014 - He graduated from Guinzadan Sur Elementary School.
  • 2015 - He entered Guinzadan Central National High School.
  • 2016 - He became an athlete, competing in a district meet and winning, also first place in the provincial meet and third in the CARAA.
  • 2018 - He moved up to senior year at Guinzadan Central National High School.
  • 2019 - When the pandemic starts, he's helping his parents in the fields by planting vegetables and looking for job like carrying stones, sand and construction work.
  • 2020 - He graduated from Guinzadan Central National High School.
  • 2021 - He enrolled criminology at Mountain Province State Polytechnic College (MPSPC) in Bontoc.
  • 2022 - dies at the age of 21.
